# 30-Day Social Media Posting Schedule

- **Duration:** 30 days (Mon-Sun for ~4 weeks)

- **Posting Frequency:** Daily on Instagram, 4-5x/week on LinkedIn

## Daily Checklist

### Morning (7-8am)
- [ ] Post scheduled content to Instagram
- [ ] Post to LinkedIn (if scheduled)
- [ ] Respond to overnight comments/DMs

### Midday (12-1pm)
- [ ] Check engagement on morning posts
- [ ] Respond to new comments
- [ ] Share post to Stories with added context

### Evening (6-8pm)
- [ ] Final comment responses
- [ ] Engage with 10 accounts in your niche
- [ ] Review next day's content

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Tasks
1. **Respond to ALL comments** within 1 hour when possible
2. **Reply with questions** to keep conversation going
3. **Like comments** from your community
4. **Share to Stories** with additional context or behind-the-scenes

### Weekly Tasks
1. **Engage with 50+ accounts** in productivity/entrepreneur niche
2. **Comment meaningfully** on 10 larger accounts
3. **Review analytics** - what's working?
4. **Adjust hashtags** based on reach data
