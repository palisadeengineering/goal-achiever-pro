POST 11
===============================================
CLARITY (0-100%)

Ask yourself:
- Can I describe my goal in one sentence?
- Do I know EXACTLY what "done" looks like?
- Is there a specific deadline?
- Can I measure progress daily?

Most people score 40% here.

Vague goals = vague results.

===============================================
POST 12
===============================================
BELIEF (0-100%)

Ask yourself:
- Do I truly believe I can achieve this?
- Have I seen others accomplish similar goals?
- Do I have the skills (or can I learn them)?
- Does my inner voice say "I will" or "I'll try"?

Low belief = self-sabotage.

===============================================
POST 13
===============================================
CONSISTENCY (0-100%)

Ask yourself:
- Am I taking action EVERY day?
- Is there dedicated time blocked for this?
- Do I have an accountability system?
- When I miss a day, do I recover quickly?

Sporadic action = sporadic results.
