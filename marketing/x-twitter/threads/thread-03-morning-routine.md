---

## Tweet 2 (The Problem)
Why most morning routines fail:

1. Too complicated (10+ steps)
2. Too long (2+ hours)
3. Too rigid (no flexibility)
4. Too aspirational (not aligned with reality)

The solution isn't another routine.

It's a SYSTEM for building one.

---

## Tweet 3 (The Principle)
The ONE principle:

"Earn the right to add complexity."

Start with a 15-minute routine.
Master it for 30 days.
Then add ONE thing.
Repeat.

Most people do the opposite.
They start with 90 minutes day one.
And quit by day 7.

---

## Tweet 4 (The Foundation)
The non-negotiable foundation (15 minutes):

1. Hydrate (2 min) - 16oz water immediately
2. Move (5 min) - Stretch, walk, anything
3. Focus (5 min) - Meditation or breathing
4. Plan (3 min) - Write 3 priorities

That's it. This is your MINIMUM.

---

## Tweet 5 (Level 2)
After 30 days, add ONE element (30 minutes total):

Options:
- Extended exercise (15 min)
- Reading (15 min)
- Journaling (15 min)
- Cold shower (5 min)

Pick what energizes YOU.
Not what some guru does.

---

## Tweet 6 (Level 3)
After 60 days, add another (45-60 minutes):

Now you've earned complexity.

My current routine:
- Hydrate + vitamins (3 min)
- Exercise (20 min)
- Cold shower (3 min)
- Meditation (10 min)
- Journal + plan (10 min)
- Reading (15 min)

Total: 61 minutes

---

## Tweet 7 (The Trap)
The biggest trap:

Copying someone else's routine.

Their routine fits:
- Their chronotype
- Their energy patterns
- Their life circumstances
- Their goals

Build YOUR routine.
For YOUR life.

---

## Tweet 8 (Energy Matching)
Match activities to energy:

HIGH energy activities:
- Exercise
- Cold exposure
- Creative work
- Learning

LOW energy activities:
- Meditation
- Journaling
- Light reading
- Gentle stretching

Know your morning energy level.
Design accordingly.

---

## Tweet 9 (Night Before)
The secret weapon: Night-before prep.

Before bed:
- Lay out workout clothes
- Prep coffee/water
- Set out journal
- Charge devices in another room

Reduce friction to near zero.
Your morning self will thank you.

---

## Tweet 10 (Recovery Protocol)
What to do when you miss:

1. Don't spiral ("I ruined my streak")
2. Do the MINIMUM (15-min foundation)
3. Reflect briefly (what happened?)
4. Reset tomorrow (fresh start)

Missing one day isn't failure.
Missing two is a pattern.

---

## Tweet 11 (The Trigger)
Use trigger stacking:

Wake up → Immediately drink water (before anything else)
Feet hit floor → Put on workout clothes
Coffee brewing → Meditate while it brews
Brush teeth → Review today's plan

Chain activities together.
Each one triggers the next.

---

## Tweet 12 (Weekend Hack)
Weekend adjustment:

Keep the FOUNDATION (15 min).
Extend or skip the extras.

This maintains the habit loop while allowing flexibility.

Rigid weekends = resentment = quitting.
