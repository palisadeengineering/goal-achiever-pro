## Tweet 3 (Framework Intro)
Value Matrix stands for:

D - Delegate
R - Replace
I - Invest

Every task you do falls into one of these 4 quadrants.

---

## Tweet 4 (Axis Explanation)
Two questions determine each task's quadrant:

1. Does this task drain or energize me?
2. Is this worth more or less than my hourly rate?

Plot every task on this matrix.

The results will shock you.

---

## Tweet 5 (D - Delegate)
D = DELEGATE

Low $ value + Drains energy

These tasks are killing your productivity:
- Email management
- Scheduling
- Data entry
- Basic admin

Action: Hire someone TODAY. Even a few hours/week changes everything.

---

## Tweet 6 (R - Replace)
R = REPLACE

High $ value + Drains energy

These tasks make money but exhaust you:
- Sales calls (that you hate)
- Client onboarding
- Project management

Action: Document, train, hand off. Short-term pain, long-term freedom.

---

## Tweet 7 (I - Invest)
I = INVEST

Low $ value + Gives energy

These tasks don't directly make money but:
- Fuel your creativity
- Maintain your health
- Build relationships
- Spark new ideas

Action: PROTECT this time. It's not optional.

---

## Tweet 8 (P - Produce)
P = PRODUCE

High $ value + Gives energy

THIS is your genius zone:
- High-level strategy
- Major deals
- Content creation
- Key relationships

Action: Maximize time here. Everything else supports this.
