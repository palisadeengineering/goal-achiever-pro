The 3 MINs

Most Important Next Steps

Every night, write down 3 MINs for tomorrow. Wake up knowing exactly what to do.

1 Maximum 3 (focus!)
2 Specific and actionable
3 Tied to a Power Goal
4 Time-blocked

X Bad MIN:

"Work on marketing"

Too vague. When is it done?

V Good MIN:

"Write 500 words of email sequence (9-10am)"

Specific. Measurable. Time-blocked.

By 10am, already won the day.
