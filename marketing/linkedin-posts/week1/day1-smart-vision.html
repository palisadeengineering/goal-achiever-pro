Vision Framework

The SMART Vision

Entrepreneurs with written, specific visions are 42% more likely to achieve their goals.

S Specific enough to measure
M Meaningful enough to pursue
A Aligned with your values
R Realistic yet stretching
T Time-bound with deadlines

Example Vision:

"By December 2027, I run a $2M/year coaching business, work 30 hours/week, and take 8 weeks of vacation annually."
