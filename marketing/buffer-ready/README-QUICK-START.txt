========================================
STEP-BY-STEP UPLOAD (15 min total)
========================================

1. OPEN BUFFER
   - Go to buffer.com
   - Click "Publishing" > "Create Post"

2. FOR EACH DAY:
   a. Open the day folder (e.g., day1/)
   b. Select ALL images in order (slide1, slide2, slide3...)
   c. Drag into Buffer's image upload area
   d. Open CAPTIONS.txt
   e. Copy the caption for that day
   f. Paste into Buffer's text field
   g. Set date/time (7:30 AM recommended)
   h. Click "Schedule" or "Add to Queue"

3. REPEAT for all 22 days
