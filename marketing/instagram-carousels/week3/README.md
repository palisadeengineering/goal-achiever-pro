## Key Messages by Day

### Day 15 - Morning Routine
- The first 2 hours determine the next 14
- Protect your morning from reactive mode
- Complete MIN #1 before checking email
- Morning is the only time you fully control

### Day 16 - Shutdown Ritual
- 15-minute non-negotiable evening process
- 5 steps: Review, Capture, Set, Check, Shutdown
- Say "Workday complete" out loud
- Tomorrow planned = tonight free

### Day 17 - Daily Reviews
- 3 reviews: Morning, Midday, Evening
- Midday review is the game-changer
- Catch problems while you can still fix them
- 20 minutes total per day

### Day 18 - Pomodoro
- Standard Pomodoro is incomplete
- Add: outcome, energy, DRIP category
- Track what drains vs energizes you
- Understand your patterns, not just time

### Day 21 - Sunday Planning
- 5 questions before bed on Sunday
- Start Monday with intention, not reaction
- Phone in another room = focus protection
- 5 minutes tonight = crushing Monday
