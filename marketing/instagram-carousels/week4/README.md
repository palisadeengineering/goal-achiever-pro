## Key Messages by Day

### Day 22 - The 4 C's
- CODE: Automation that works while you sleep
- CONTENT: Create once, reach forever
- CAPITAL: Money doing the work
- COLLABORATION: Other people's audiences
- Ask "How do I leverage this?" instead of "How do I do this?"

### Day 23 - Delegation
- The $500 decision that made $50,000
- Calculate true cost: hours x hourly rate
- Delegation is an investment, not an expense
- Better results when specialists do the work

### Day 24 - Friend Inventory
- Rate relationships on Energy, Value, Frequency
- Four categories: Energizers, Investors, Drainers, Ghosts
- Double down on energizers
- Reconnect with ghosts TODAY

### Day 25 - Full System
- Complete hierarchy: Vision -> Power Goals -> Targets -> Actions
- Execution layer: Time blocks, Reviews
- Supporting systems: Routines, 4 C's, Network
- It's about doing the RIGHT things consistently

### Day 30 - Take Action
- Month recap of all 4 weeks
- The only question: Did you take action?
- Pick ONE thing to implement
- Final CTA to try the app
