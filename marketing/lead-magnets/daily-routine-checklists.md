## Morning, Midday & Evening Routines for Peak Performance

*Based on high-performer research and time optimization methodology*

## Why Routines Matter

> "You'll never change your life until you change something you do daily." - John Maxwell

Research shows:
- **Morning routines** set the tone for your entire day
- **Midday routines** reset your energy and focus
- **Evening routines** ensure quality sleep and next-day preparation

The goal: Create **automatic systems** that make success inevitable.

## Part 1: The Morning Routine

### The Golden Hour (First 60 Minutes)

Your first hour sets the direction of your entire day. Protect it fiercely.

#### The M.I.R.A.C.L.E. Morning Framework

| Letter | Activity | Duration | Purpose |
|--------|----------|----------|---------|
| **M** | Meditation | 10 min | Calm mind, reduce stress |
| **I** | Intentions | 5 min | Set daily priorities |
| **R** | Reading | 15 min | Learn and grow |
| **A** | Affirmations | 5 min | Program positive beliefs |
| **C** | Cardio/Exercise | 20 min | Energy and health |
| **L** | Log/Journal | 5 min | Reflect and plan |
| **E** | Eat Well | - | Fuel your body |

**Total:** ~60 minutes

## Part 2: The Midday Reset

### Why Midday Matters

Research shows:
- Energy naturally dips after lunch
- Decision fatigue accumulates
- Stress builds through the day
- A reset prevents afternoon slump

---

### The Power Reset (15-30 minutes)

#### Quick Reset (15 minutes)
- [ ] Step away from workspace (2 min)
- [ ] Breathwork: 4-7-8 breathing x 5 rounds (5 min)
- [ ] Light walk or stretch (5 min)
- [ ] Hydrate (water + snack if needed)
- [ ] Review afternoon priorities (3 min)

#### Deep Reset (30 minutes)
- [ ] Leave workspace completely (walk outside if possible)
- [ ] Eat mindfully (no screens)
- [ ] 10-minute meditation or nap
- [ ] Stretch or light movement
- [ ] Review and re-prioritize afternoon
- [ ] Clear any urgent messages

---

## Routine Building Tips

### Start Small
- Don't add all elements at once
- Add one habit at a time
- Master each before adding more

### Be Consistent
- Same time every day
- 7 days a week (weekends too)
- Minimum 21 days to form habit

### Prepare the Night Before
- Lay out workout clothes
- Prep coffee/breakfast
- Set out journal/book
- Charge devices in another room

### Create Triggers
- Wake up → immediately drink water
- Coffee brewing → meditate
- Dinner done → evening walk
- Brush teeth → journal

### Handle Disruptions
- Travel routine (modified version)
- Sick day routine (absolute minimum)
- Weekend routine (slightly relaxed)

1. **Acknowledge** - "I missed today, and that's okay"

3. **Reset Tomorrow** - Plan for perfect execution
4. **No Guilt** - Progress over perfection
