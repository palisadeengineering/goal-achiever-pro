# 7-Day Time Audit Template

**Track where your time actually goes. Discover 10-20+ hours you're wasting.**

---

## How to Use This Template

### Step 1: Track Every 15 Minutes
For the next 7 days, log what you do in 15-minute blocks. Be brutally honest - write what you actually did, not what you planned to do.

### Step 2: Rate Your Energy (1-5)
After each block, rate how you felt:
- ⚡⚡⚡⚡⚡ (5) = Peak state, crushing it
- ⚡⚡⚡⚡ (4) = High energy, focused
- ⚡⚡⚡ (3) = Neutral, getting by
- ⚡⚡ (2) = Sluggish, forcing it
- ⚡ (1) = Running on fumes

### Step 3: Categorize Using the Value Matrix
At the end of each day, categorize each block:
- **D** = Delegation (drains you, someone else should do it)
- **R** = Replacement (you're good at it, but shouldn't do it)
- **I** = Investment (builds future capacity)
- **P** = Production (your highest-value work)

### Step 4: Analyze Your Week
Calculate your percentages and identify patterns.
