## What Are Power Goals?

Power Goals are 12 strategic projects you commit to completing over the next 12 months - one per month. Unlike vague resolutions, Power Goals are:

- **Specific** - Clear, measurable outcomes
- **Time-bound** - One month per goal
- **Strategic** - Aligned with your vision
- **Achievable** - Realistic but stretching

> "Most people overestimate what they can do in a day and underestimate what they can do in a year." - Bill Gates

## Common Power Goal Mistakes

### Mistake #1: Too Many Goals
**Problem:** Trying to do 3 Power Goals in one month
**Solution:** ONE Power Goal per month, total focus

### Mistake #2: Too Vague
**Problem:** "Get healthy" or "Grow business"
**Solution:** Specific, measurable outcomes with deadlines

### Mistake #3: No Weekly Milestones
**Problem:** Leaving everything until the last week
**Solution:** Break into 4 weekly chunks

### Mistake #4: Not Connected to Vision
**Problem:** Goals that don't move you toward your 3-year vision
**Solution:** Start with vision, then create aligned goals

### Mistake #5: No Accountability
**Problem:** Only you know about the goal
**Solution:** Share with accountability partner or community
